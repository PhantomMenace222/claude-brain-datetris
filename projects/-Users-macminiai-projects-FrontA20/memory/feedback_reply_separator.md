Start every reply with a blank line followed by `---` on its own line before any other content.

**Why:** User finds it easier to visually scan and distinguish reply boundaries in the transcript.

**How to apply:** Every assistant turn, no exceptions — including short acknowledgements, tool-only responses, and end-of-turn summaries. The `---` goes at the very top of the message, before any text or tool-use narration.
